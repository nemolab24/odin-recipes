Shoarma

Description

This shoarma recipes makes tasty, grilled shoarma for 4.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breasts
  • Pita Bread
  • Greek Yoghurt
  • Lots of garlic

Steps

  1. marinate chicken in some shoarma seasonings and Yoghurt
  2. make garlic sauce by combining the yoghurt with mayonaisse and garlic. Add salt and sugar to taste.
  3. Grill the chciken and Pita bread in seperate pans
  4. Assemble the Pita with the chicken and the saue
