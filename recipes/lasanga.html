Lasanga

Description

this recipes makes a smooth lasagne with lots of cheese. it makes 4 portions.

Ingredients

  • Lasagne Pasta
  • Bolognese Sauce
  • Bechamel Sauce
  • Ground Beef

Step

  1. First cook the Bolognese Sauce to your taste
  2. Cook the Pasta el-dente
  3. Pan fry the ground beef with seasonings
  4. Make the Bechemel Sauce
  5. Put everything together, and put in the oven
