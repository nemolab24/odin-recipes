Double Chocolate Cookie

Description

This recipes makes a soft,chocolaty, sweet Cookie.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Brown sugar
  • White sugar
  • Vanilla
  • All-purpose Flour
  • an egg

Steps

  1. WHisk butter until smooth and fluffy
  2. Add the sugar and whisk until smooth again
  3. Add the egg and whisk calmly, dont overmix
  4. add flour and vanilla and mix unitl combined
  5. put in pre-heated oven at 180 degrees celsius for 10 minutes
